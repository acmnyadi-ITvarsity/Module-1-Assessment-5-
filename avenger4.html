Backstory

The man who would become known as Hawkeye was born Clint Barton. Orphaned at an early age, he joined the circus and apprenticed himself to the Swordsman, a performer who specialized in tricks with blades. After he dicovered the Swordsman stealing from the circus, the two fought and Barton was left for dead.
